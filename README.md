## About

I'm a **founder, operator, and investor** with a track record of building ventures from zero to one — across software, AI, and digital strategy. I don't just write code — I build companies, teams, and the systems that scale them.

I founded my first company at **16**. Today I lead **Zulbera**, a software studio shaping custom SaaS platforms, AI-driven products, and brand systems for clients across industries — while holding active leadership roles in fitness technology and cybersecurity, and a growing portfolio of startup investments.

My edge sits at the intersection of **technology**, **design**, and **business strategy** — turning raw ideas into market-ready companies, and market-ready companies into compounding ventures.

<br/>

> *"Most people wait for the right moment. I build it."*
